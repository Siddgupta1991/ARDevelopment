AR Physics Lab - Marker

Print this marker and place it on your table

Instructions:

  1. Print this page - Make sure the marker is about 8cm x 8cm in size
  2. Cut out the marker and place it on a flat surface
  3. Open the AR app on your phone
  4. Point your phone camera at the marker
  5. Keep the marker well-lit and avoid shadows
  6. Hold the phone steady about 20-30cm above the marker

Note: The black border is essential for detection. Don't cut it off!
